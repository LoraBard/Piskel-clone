Piskel - clone

Piskel is a free online editor for animated sprites & pixel art

Create animations in your browser. use Google sign in to access your gallery or simply create a new sprite.

Example sprites

  • Live preview

    Check a preview of your animation in real time as you draw. Adjust the frame delay on the fly.

  • Open source

    All the code is open-source and available on GitHub .

  • Export to GIF, APNG...

    Several export modes supported. Animated GIFs for sharing, spritesheet PNG/ZIP for bigger projects etc…

  • Upload to google drive

    Upload files from your computer to google drive
